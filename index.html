About US

Over With Deceptions is an independent band hailing from the vibrant music scenes of Las Piñas and Bacoor, Cavite. Formed out of a shared passion for honest storytelling and raw sound, the band blends elements of alternative rock, indie, and post-grunge to create music that speaks to real emotions and everyday struggles. Our songs explore themes of love, loss, growth, and self-discovery reflections of the experiences that shape who we are. Whether performing in small gigs or larger stages, we aim to connect with listeners through authenticity and energy that goes beyond the stage. Over With Deceptions isn’t just a band name it’s a statement about facing truth and breaking free from illusions. Through our music, we hope to inspire others to stay true to themselves and find strength in vulnerability.
